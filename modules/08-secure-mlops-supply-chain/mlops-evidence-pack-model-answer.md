## Core lesson

Students should learn that MLOps supply-chain review is about evidence. The key question is not "did the model get a good metric?" The key question is whether the organization can prove what was promoted, where it came from, who approved it, what evidence supported the decision, and how the team can recover if the decision was wrong.
