# AI Red Team Labs

These labs support Module 11  -  AI Red Team Methodology.

They are designed as controlled, fake-data, local/tabletop exercises. The goal is not to attack real systems. The goal is to practice structured AI red team planning, evidence collection, reporting, and remediation design.

## Labs

| Lab | Purpose |
|---|---|
| [`ai-red-team-scoping-tabletop.md`](ai-red-team-scoping-tabletop.md) | Define scope, rules of engagement, safety boundaries, evidence requirements, and success criteria. |
| [`brokenpilot-attack-chain-lab.md`](brokenpilot-attack-chain-lab.md) | Plan and document a realistic attack chain against the BrokenPilot capstone design. |

## Expected deliverables

Students should produce:

- rules of engagement;
- system-understanding notes;
- attack paths;
- evidence plan;
- one or more findings;
- severity rationale;
- remediation plan;
- executive summary;
- residual-risk statement.
