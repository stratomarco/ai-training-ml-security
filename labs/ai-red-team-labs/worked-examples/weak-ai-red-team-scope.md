# Weak Example: AI Red Team Scope

Try to break the AI assistant and see what happens. Test prompt injection, data leaks, and tool use. Write down anything interesting.

## Why this is weak

- No objective is defined.
- No legal or safety boundaries are stated.
- It does not define valid evidence.
- It does not distinguish in-scope from out-of-scope systems.
- It encourages bug hunting instead of decision-grade security testing.
