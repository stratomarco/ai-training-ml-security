# Weak Executive Readout Example — BrokenPilot

BrokenPilot has prompt injection and can leak data. This is dangerous. We should not launch until all OWASP issues are fixed. The AI needs better guardrails and should be monitored.

## Why this is weak

Problems:

- No business context.
- No prioritization.
- No distinction between pilot and full launch.
- No concrete controls.
- No decision recommendation beyond generic blocking.
- No residual risk discussion.

A strong executive readout should help leadership make a decision.
