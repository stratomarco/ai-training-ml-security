## How to use these references

Use OWASP to structure risk categories, MITRE ATLAS to map adversarial tactics and techniques, NIST AI RMF to frame risk management and governance, and BIML-style architectural risk analysis to connect red team findings back to design decisions.

The goal is not to cite frameworks mechanically. The goal is to use them to make findings clearer, more consistent, and more actionable.
