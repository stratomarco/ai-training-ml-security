- Repackaging BrokenPilot or other labs as a paid product
- Selling certification, exam preparation, or courseware based primarily on this project

## Internal organizational learning

Internal employee learning is allowed under the additional permission described in `LICENSE.md`, `LICENSE-CONTENT.md`, and `LICENSE-CODE.md`, provided that attribution is preserved and the material is not sold, repackaged, or delivered as a paid external offering.
