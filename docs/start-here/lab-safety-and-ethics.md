# Lab Safety and Ethics

All labs should be performed only in controlled environments that you own or are explicitly authorized to test.

Do not use course techniques against public systems, third-party AI services, production applications, customer data, or systems where you do not have explicit permission.

## Lab rules

- Use fake data.
- Use local or intentionally vulnerable training targets.
- Do not test external systems without written authorization.
- Do not attempt to exfiltrate real secrets or personal data.
- Keep evidence limited to the training environment.
- Focus on root cause, impact, mitigation, and residual risk.

## Purpose

The goal is to teach security reasoning and defensive design, not to enable misuse.
