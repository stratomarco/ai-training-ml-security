# License

This repository uses a split-license model.

The intent is simple:

- Individuals may read, study, and use the course for self-learning.
- Educators and study groups may use the course for non-commercial learning.
- Organizations may use the course internally for employee learning if they preserve attribution and do not sell, repackage, or productize the material.
- Anyone who wants to sell training, consulting, workshops, courseware, platforms, or products based on this work must request a commercial license first.

## Additional permission for internal organizational learning

The maintainers grant an additional permission for organizations to use this material internally for employee learning, awareness, study groups, and non-public training, provided that:

1. The material is not sold as a course, workshop, certification, platform, consulting deliverable, or commercial product.
2. The organization does not charge attendees or customers for access to the material.
3. Attribution to this project is preserved.
4. Modified versions are clearly marked as modified.
5. The material is not presented as the organization's original work.
6. Runnable labs are used only with fake data and controlled local environments.

This additional permission does not allow commercial resale, paid external training, paid consulting delivery, paid platform use, or repackaging as a monetized product.
