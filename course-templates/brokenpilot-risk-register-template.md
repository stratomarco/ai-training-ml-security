## Severity guidance

| Severity | Guidance |
|---|---|
| Critical | Broad access, high-impact action, sensitive data exposure, or unsafe workflow change with little friction. |
| High | Significant data exposure or action misuse with realistic access. |
| Medium | Limited blast radius or meaningful compensating controls. |
| Low | Low-impact issue or difficult-to-exploit condition. |
| Informational | Useful observation that does not create direct risk yet. |

## Notes

Focus on business-relevant risk. Do not create separate risks for every prompt variation if the root cause is the same.
