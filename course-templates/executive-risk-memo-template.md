# Executive Risk Memo Template

Use this template when you need to communicate an AI or ML security issue to leadership. The goal is to support a decision, not to list every technical detail.

## Decision needed

State the decision in one sentence.

Example:

> Decide whether the internal AI operations assistant should proceed to a limited pilot next week.

## Recommendation

- Proceed

- Delay until launch blockers are fixed
- Do not proceed
