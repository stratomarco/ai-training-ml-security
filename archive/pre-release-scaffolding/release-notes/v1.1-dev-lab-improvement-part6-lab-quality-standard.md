# v1.1-dev Lab Improvement Part 6: Lab Quality Standard and Anchors

This update raises lab quality rather than adding a new attack feature.

## Added

- Shared lab quality standard.
- Reasoning lab deliverable template.
- Reasoning lab quality rubric.
- Strong and weak anchors for membership inference, poisoning/backdoor tabletop, DocOps architecture review, and AI red-team scoping.
- Foundation deliverable anchors for Modules 01 and 02.
- RAG and agent lab consolidation notes pointing students to BrokenPilot as the runnable path.

## Intent

Attack labs should be observable when that is the right modality. Reasoning labs should not be forced into fake runnable demos. They should instead produce decision-grade artifacts with evidence, root cause, controls, validation, and residual risk.
