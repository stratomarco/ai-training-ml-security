# Capstone Grading Rubric

## Total score: 100 points

| Area | Points |
|---|---:|
| Architecture understanding | 10 |
| Asset and trust-boundary identification | 15 |
| Threat model quality | 15 |
| Vulnerability discovery | 15 |
| Exploit explanation | 10 |
| Mitigation quality | 15 |
| Residual risk reasoning | 10 |
| Communication quality | 10 |

## Architecture understanding

Strong submissions clearly describe the system, components, data flows, trust boundaries, tools, and user roles.

## Threat model quality

Strong submissions identify realistic attackers, abuse cases, assumptions, assets, and control gaps.

## Vulnerability discovery

Strong submissions find vulnerabilities across multiple categories, not only prompt injection.

## Mitigation quality

Strong submissions propose practical controls that can be implemented by engineering teams.

## Residual risk reasoning

Strong submissions explain what remains unsafe and who should own the risk.

## Communication quality

Strong submissions explain findings clearly to both engineers and leadership.
